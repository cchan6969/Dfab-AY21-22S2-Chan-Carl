Lazer Cutting

For the music box, I have used Autodesk Inventor Professional 2020 to design.

Then I exported the faces as .dxf files and opened in Inkscape.

As a proof of concept, I have attempted to engrave Mai Sakurajima from 青春ブタ野郎はランドセルガールの夢を見ない into the inside cover of the music box.
